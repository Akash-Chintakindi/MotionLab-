MotionLab — Learning Science & Progression Rules
================================================

Core principle
--------------
Students advance through the course by demonstrating mastery, not just by
clicking through content. Each topic has three modes — Learn, Practice, and
Quiz — and progression is gated by performance.

The two thresholds
------------------
1. LESSON MASTERY = 80%+
   - A topic's Learn section must be completed with at least 80% mastery
     (i.e. ≥ 80% of the gradable steps answered correctly on the first try).
   - Reaching 80%+ on Learn UNLOCKS that topic's Practice game and Quiz.
   - If a student scores below 80% on Learn, Practice and Quiz stay LOCKED.
     The student can review what they missed and redo the lesson (with fresh
     practice questions once the AI generator / expanded question bank is wired
     in) until they reach mastery.

2. QUIZ PASS = 70%+
   - A student must score at least 70% on a topic's Quiz to move on.
   - Scoring 70%+ on the Quiz UNLOCKS the next topic/lesson.
   - Scoring below 70% means the student must redo the quiz; the next topic
     stays locked until they pass.

Why these numbers
-----------------
- 80% on Learn ensures the student genuinely understands the core idea before
  spending time on the game and the graded quiz — practice is more valuable when
  the fundamentals are solid.
- 70% on the Quiz is a standard "passing" bar: it confirms competence without
  demanding perfection, while still blocking students who haven't yet learned
  the material from racing ahead and compounding gaps.

Progression flow per topic
--------------------------
  Learn  →  (≥80%?) → unlock Practice + Quiz
                       │
                       └─ <80% → review answers / redo Learn

  Quiz   →  (≥70%?) → unlock NEXT topic
                       │
                       └─ <70% → redo Quiz (next topic stays locked)

Mastery is stored as the student's BEST attempt, so once a topic is unlocked it
stays unlocked even if a later replay scores lower.
